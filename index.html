About Me

I am a Ph.D student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at McGill University. I am working at Systems and Control Group, Center for Intelligent Machines (CIM). I am affiliated with Montreal Institute of Learning Algorithms (MILA). I am supervised by Professor Aditya Mahajan and Co-supervised by Professor Jerome Le Ny.

I was a former Research Scientist intern at Aerial Technologies were I focussed on learning algorithms for real-time indoor localization using wireless signals. I was a former Research Engineer at the Department of Biomedical Physics and Technology University of Dhaka under the supervision of Professor Siddique-e-Rabbani. I completed my Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from McGill University and my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Islamic University of Technology.

Research Interest

I am primarily interested in using Machine Learning algorithms for achieving optimal control of multi-agent systems. My research focus is therefore at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning and Control Systems for intelligent control applications. I work with applications of human automation team problem. Furthermore, I am interested in domains of RL/ MDPs for scalable navigation and manipulation, especially novel algorithms that can be deployed on humanoid robots.
